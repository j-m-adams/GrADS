q shades

q shades

Lists the colors and levels of shaded plots.This command takes no arguments.

 

Examples

ga-> set gxout shaded
ga-> d t2m
Contouring: 220 to 300 interval 10 
ga-> q shades
Number of levels = 10
9 <= 220
14 220 230
4 230 240
5 240 250
13 250 260
10 260 270
7 270 280
8 280 290
2 290 300
6 300 >
